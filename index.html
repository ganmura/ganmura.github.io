About me

Masahiro Minowa [箕輪 昌紘]

I'm a glaciologist working at ILTS in Hokkaido University. I combine field observations (GPS, seismisity, tsunami waves, time-lapse imagery, ice radar...) with remote sensing dataset to understand the glacier mass change under the current climate. Now I'm working on developing a glacier model based on the observational dataset.
